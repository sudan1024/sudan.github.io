Biography

I am currently a search scientist at LARC, Singapore Management University (SMU). My supervisor is Prof. Jing Jiang and my secondary supervisor is Prof. Feida Zhu. I received my Bachelor degree from School of Mathematics and Statistics, Southwest University (Chongqing, China).

I have wide interests in applying deep learning methods to some NLP tasks like Knowledge Base Question Answering, Textual Entailment, Knowledge Base Completion etc..
